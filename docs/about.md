# About

Caribbean Healthcare Interoperability Architecture is an open and collaborative initiative to define a shared architecture for cross-border healthcare interoperability in the Caribbean.

## Why CHIA

Healthcare in the Caribbean increasingly requires collaboration across borders. However, digital health systems are often fragmented, making it difficult to securely and effectively share information. CHIA aims to provide a common foundation to address these challenges.

## Community

CHIA is community-driven and brings together healthcare professionals, architects, policymakers, and technologists interested in improving cross-border healthcare.

## Status

CHIA is currently in an early stage and is evolving based on feedback and collaboration across the region.
