# Use Case: Cross-Border Emergency Care

## Scenario
A patient from Country A is treated in an emergency department in Country B.

## Problem today
- No access to medical history  
- Risk of medical errors  
- Delays in treatment  

## Desired situation
- Clinician can access relevant patient data  
- Data is trusted and up to date  
- Care can be delivered safely and quickly  

## Required capabilities
- Patient identification across borders  
- Secure data exchange  
- Access to summary health record  

## Related building blocks
- Patient Identity  
- Consent Management  
- Health Data Exchange  

## Impact
- Improved patient safety  
- Faster treatment  
- Better outcomes
